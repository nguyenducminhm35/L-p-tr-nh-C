The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cvottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back foer centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estata gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetable and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
